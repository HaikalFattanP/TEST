ABOUT AFESTA VOL 4

Celebrating a decade of student artistry. MusicFest is more than a concert; it’s a collision of raw energy, creative rebellion, and the purest expression of sound crafted by the next generation of performers.

The Evolution

In 2014, three students with a beat-up amp and a dream started what would become the school's biggest annual event. From a garage gathering to a main-stage spectacle, MusicFest has grown into a decade-long legacy.
